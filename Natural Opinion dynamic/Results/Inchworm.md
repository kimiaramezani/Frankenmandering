# Description
In this report, two experiments are executed: 
Experiment 1: In this experiment, each node is located in its own district, and the Normalization Factor equals 1.
Experiment 2: In this experiment, nodes 0, 1, and 5 are in one district, and the other nodes are located in their own districts. The normalization factor is also equal to 1.

DRF 1 = f1  
Drf2 = DRF with no social network     
DRF3 = DRF with social network
